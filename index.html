About

The Beach Boys is an American rock band formed in Hawthorne, California, in 1961. They have released 29 studio albums, eight live albums, 55 compilation albums, and 71 singles. The current members are Brian Wilson, Mike Love, Al Jardine and Bruce Johnston.

From humble roots in California, The Beach Boys have become an international sensation. Their famous harmonies and classic songs continue to travel the world bringing “Good Vibrations” everywhere. With over 50 years experience performing live around the world, the band bring amazing energy and vibrance to any event. To request a booking, please go to the contact section.
